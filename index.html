Protocol & Systems

Zone 2 Cardio

Fat Loss Engine

Frequency: Tue, Thu, Sat (35–40 mins)

Rules: Flat surfaces only. Conversational pace.

  • Burns fat, keeps cortisol low, aids recovery.
  • Avoid jump rope, stairs, and steep inclines.
  • Progress from flat brisk walking to light jogging over weeks.

Diet Target

Deficit

Goal: 300–500 kcal daily deficit.

Protein: 1.8–2.0g per kg of bodyweight.

  • Biggest lever: Cut cooking oil usage by half.
  • Carbs timed around workouts; lower on cardio days.
  • Hydration: 3L+ water daily.

🎯 Muscle-Up Progression Roadmap

Track your KPIs until you hit the target.

Level 1: Pulling Strength

Target: 8–10 clean pull-ups. Focus on volume, dead hangs, and body control.

Level 2: Explosive Power

Target: 10+ pull-ups + 15+ dips. Focus on pulling the bar to your hips (chest-to-bar).

Level 3: Transition

Target: Strong dip press and bar reaching the hip on the pull. Practice band-assisted reps.

Level 4: The Muscle-Up

Execute strict, clean, and controlled. (You are ready for attempts when you can hit 10 strict pull-ups and 15 strict dips.)
