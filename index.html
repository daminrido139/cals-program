DAY 1 • STRENGTH

Monday: Upper Body (Pull Focus)

Time: 45-60 min Type: Pull/Push
EXERCISE TARGET REP RANGE REST COACH NOTES
Band-Assisted Pull-ups 3 Sets x 8-10 reps 90 - 120s Use a resistance band that allows perfect range of motion.
Standard Push-ups 3 Sets x 10-15 reps 90s Keep glutes/core tight. Tightly tuck elbows at 45-deg.
Inverted Rows 3 Sets x 10-12 reps 90s Hang under a low bar. Pull chest up to touch bar.
Assisted Dips 3 Sets x 8-10 reps 90 - 120s Use machine or bands to protect shoulder rotators.
DAY 2 • ATHLETIC

Tuesday: Lower Body & Core Focus

Time: 45 min Type: Legs/Abs
EXERCISE TARGET REP RANGE REST COACH NOTES
Bulgarian Split Squats 3 Sets x 10 reps/leg 90s Elevate rear foot on bench. Hold light dumbbells if easy.
Jump Squats 3 Sets x 15 reps 90s Explode upwards high. Soft landings on toes.
Hanging Knee Raises 3 Sets x 10-15 reps 60s Hang from pull-up bar. Bring knees up towards chest.
L-Sit Practice (Tuck) Accumulate 60s total As needed Perform on gym parallel bars. Hold tucked knees dynamic.
DAY 3 • RECOVERY PROTOCOL

Wednesday: Light Active Recovery & Mobility

Time: 30-40 min Type: Mobility/LISS
Why active recovery? Wednesday intercepts your week to flash-flush out cellular metabolic lactic waste and lubricate muscle tendons. Running a calorie deficit creates nervous stress; keep this workout strictly low-intensity to recharge.
EXERCISE BLOCK DURATION / PROTOCOL INTENSITY LEVEL GOAL ACTION
LISS Cardio (Incline Treadmill Walk) 25 - 30 Minutes Low (Heart rate < 120 bpm) Keeps metabolism high while burning pure fat without taxing muscles.
Dead Hangs 3 Sets x 30-45 seconds Gentle Decompression Decompresses spine; lubricates shoulders for pullups.
Shoulder Dislocates (with Band) 2 Sets x 15 reps Smooth Rotation Increases shoulder socket rotation capability.
DAY 4 • HYPERTROPHY

Thursday: Upper Body (Push Focus)

Time: 50 min Type: Push/Pull
EXERCISE TARGET REP RANGE REST COACH NOTES
Pike Push-ups 3 Sets x 6-10 reps 90s Keep hips high, forming an inverted V shape. Builds shoulders.
Slow Eccentric Pull-up Negatives 3 Sets x 5 reps 120s Jump to top. Lower down over a 5-second countdown.
Diamond Push-ups 3 Sets x 10-15 reps 90s Hands closer together under sternum. Blasts the triceps.
Underhand Grip Chin-ups 3 Sets x Max reps (then band) 90s Target bicep pull raw power. Use bands for extra reps.
DAY 5 • LEGS & CORE

Friday: Lower Body & Midsection Volume

Time: 45 min Type: Conditioning
EXERCISE TARGET REP RANGE REST COACH NOTES
Goblet Squats (with Dumbbell) 3 Sets x 12 reps 90s Hold dumbbell vertically under chin. Keep spine upright.
Walking Lunges 3 Sets x 20 total steps 90s Take large strides, driving weight through front heel.
Forearm Plank 3 Sets x 60 seconds 60s Squeeze core and glutes horizontally straight.
Standing Calf Raises 3 Sets x 20 reps 60s Execute on step or plate for deep lower stretching.
DAY 6 • OUTDOOR / OFF-CAMPUS

Saturday: Outdoor LISS Recovery Walk & Core Decompress

Time: 40-50 min Type: LISS Cardio
Fat burning peak: Saturday keeps calorie expenditures elevated. A fasted morning walk maximizes your lipolysis (fat cell burning) since your liver glycogen stores are low from sleeping.
ACTIVITY BLOCK TARGET QUANTITY INTENSITY LEVEL GOAL ACTION
Outdoors Flat Walk (Fasted Preferred) 35 - 45 Minutes (or ~7,000 steps) Easy, conversational pace Burns high ratio of fatty acids directly. Excellent mental break.
Active Hamstring & Hip Stretching 10 - 15 Minutes Mild stretch intensity Releases tight hamstring/hips from Tuesday and Friday squats.

Time: 24h Type: Zero Work

Sunday Rest Mandate

No calisthenics, no weighted movements, no high-intensity work. Let your central nervous system (CNS) completely restabilize and recharge.
