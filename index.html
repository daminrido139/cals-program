🏋️ Road to Muscle-Up

Functional Athletic Development | 7-Day Split

About This Program

Attribute Detail
Goal Functional, athletic body + Muscle-Up mastery
Starting Level 3–4 pull-ups
Gym Days Monday – Friday (pull-up bar, dip bars, barbells, dumbbells, parallettes)
Home Days Saturday & Sunday (bodyweight only; doorframe pull-up bar recommended)
Timeline 12–20 weeks with consistent effort
Philosophy: Calisthenics rewards patience and consistency over ego. Every single rep — especially the ugly ones — is a deposit into your future muscle-up.

Progression Phases

Phase Weeks Theme
Foundation 1–4 Build base pulling strength, master movement patterns, negative pull-ups
Strength 5–8 Increase pull-up volume, chest-to-bar work, dip loading
Skill 9–12 Explosive pull-ups, transition drills, band-assisted muscle-up
Execution 12+ Full muscle-up attempts, strict form

Day 4 — Thursday: Power Clean & Jerk + Cond.

Your favourite movement. The power clean & jerk demands explosive hip drive, coordination, and speed. Treat it with respect.

Warm-Up (15 min)

  • Empty bar: Hang cleans (3×5), Cleans to knee (2×5)

└── Strict, clean, controlled
    └── Then: Multiple reps, weighted, rings

Rules, Nutrition & Tracking

Progression Rules

  1. Pull-ups: Add 1 rep to best set every 2 weeks. If stalled for 3 weeks, add 1 negative-only set.
  2. Push-ups: Progress to harder variants when 3 × 20 is clean.
  3. Dips: Add a loaded backpack when 3 × 15 feels comfortable.
  4. Olympic lifts: Never compromise technique. Deload 10% if ugly.
  5. Deload: Every 4th week — cut volume by 40%.
