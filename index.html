2. The Goal — Straight Talk [cite: 1]

Target: 26.3% → 12% body fat in 20 weeks. [cite: 1]

Here's the honest version: this is aggressive, not impossible. [cite: 1] To hit 12% while keeping (and slightly building) muscle, you need roughly 10–11 kg of fat loss in 140 days — about 0.5–0.6 kg/week, sustained without major breaks. [cite: 1] That's on the upper edge of what's sustainable for someone your size (68 kg). [cite: 1] It is achievable if: [cite: 1]

  • Diet adherence is close to 100%, not "most days" [cite: 1]
  • Training is consistent — no multi-week gaps like your last month [cite: 1]
  • You track weekly average weight, not daily noise [cite: 1]

If you nail this, expect to land at 58–60 kg somewhere around 12–14% body fat, with visibly more muscle than you have now (because you'll be building some of it concurrently — a real advantage of being a beginner-to-intermediate lifter). 5. The Path to Your First Muscle-Up (4 Phases) [cite: 1]

Phase 1 — Foundation (Weeks 1–4) [cite: 1]

Goal: 4–5 strict pull-ups, clean full dips, training habit locked in. [cite: 1]

  • Pull-ups: negatives (5-sec lower) 4×5, band-assisted or machine-assisted 4×8-10, scap pulls 3×10, dead hangs 3×30-45s [cite: 1]
  • Dips: assisted/bench dips → full dips, 4×8-10 [cite: 1]
  • Gym accessory (hypertrophy): lat pulldown, seated row, incline DB press, lateral raises — 3×10-12 each [cite: 1]
  • Legs: goblet squat or leg press, RDL, walking lunges — 3×10-12 [cite: 1]
  • Core: hollow hold, plank, hanging knee raise — 3×30-45s or 12-15 reps [cite: 1]

Phase 2 — Strength & Volume (Weeks 5–10) [cite: 1]

Goal: 8 strict pull-ups, 15+ dips, 3-5 chest-to-bar reps. [cite: 1]

  • Pull-ups: max-effort sets 5×5 @ RPE8, add small weight once 8+ reps unassisted is easy [cite: 1]
  • Dips: push to 15-20 reps, begin light weighted dips [cite: 1]
  • Add tuck L-sit holds (3×15-20s) — core compression strength needed for the muscle-up [cite: 1]
  • Explosive/chest-to-bar pull-ups 3×5 for power [cite: 1]
  • Continue gym hypertrophy work for shoulders/arms/back thickness [cite: 1]

Phase 3 — Muscle-Up Transition (Weeks 11–16) [cite: 1]

Goal: first muscle-up (band-assisted is a completely valid milestone). [cite: 1]

  • False grip hangs 3×20-30s — this is the technical key most people skip [cite: 1]
  • Muscle-up transition drills (jump negatives, band-assisted reps) [cite: 1]
  • Chest-to-bar pull-ups 4×3-5, aiming for higher pull each week [cite: 1]
  • Advanced L-sit progression, tuck front lever holds [cite: 1]
  • Weighted dips continue progressing [cite: 1]

Phase 4 — Peak & Polish (Weeks 17-20) [cite: 1]

Goal: strict muscle-up reps, finish the cut looking sharp. [cite: 1]

  • Strict muscle-up practice, build toward multiple reps [cite: 1]
  • Aesthetic finishers: arm/shoulder isolation work, ab circuits [cite: 1]
  • Cardio finishers 2x/week if fat loss has stalled [cite: 1]
  • Week 19 or 20: deload — cut volume ~40-50% to arrive fresh for your final assessment [cite: 1]
  • Final InBody scan + photos, week 20 [cite: 1]

8. Tracking & Adjustment Protocol (Important — Don't Skip) [cite: 1]

  1. Weigh yourself daily, same time, same conditions (morning, after washroom, before food). [cite: 1] Track the weekly average, not single-day numbers. [cite: 1]
  2. InBody scan every 2 weeks (you clearly have easy access — use it). [cite: 1]
  3. If weekly average isn't dropping ~0.4-0.6 kg for 2 straight weeks → cut 100-150 kcal from intake. [cite: 1]
  4. If you're dropping faster than 0.8 kg/week → add 100-150 kcal back. [cite: 1] Fast loss at your bodyweight risks muscle loss, not just fat. [cite: 1]
  5. Every 4 weeks, reassess pull-up/dip numbers and adjust phase progression up or down based on actual performance, not the calendar. [cite: 1]

9. Recovery [cite: 1]

  • Sleep: 7-8 hours minimum — this is not optional in a deficit while building strength; skimping here undercuts both fat loss and recovery. [cite: 1]
  • Full rest day (Sunday) stays a full rest day even when motivated — recovery is where the muscle-up strength actually gets built. [cite: 1]
